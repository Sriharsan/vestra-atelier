# Client Walkthrough — Vestra Atelier

A plain tour of every page and the fitting room, written for a non-technical reader.

---

## The homepage

When visitors arrive at vestra.ai, they see a warm, editorial-style page that tells the Vestra story in a single scroll.

**Hero.** A large serif headline, "See every look, before you buy," sits beside an image of a model wearing a Vestra-rendered outfit. Two buttons invite the visitor to try the dressing room or learn about the brand platform.

**The problem.** A split section showing that 64% of fashion returns are fit-related and that the UK alone sees GBP 7 billion in returned fashion annually. This frames why Vestra exists.

**The solution.** A short editorial block: "Give the shopper a mirror that works from anywhere." One photograph, every garment rendered in seconds.

**How it works.** Three numbered steps: (1) the shopper uploads a single photograph, (2) the garment is draped using cloth-aware physics, (3) the shopper sees the full styled look.

**Lookbook.** Three sample rendered looks — Saffron Sunlit, Clay and Sage, Atelier Espresso — each showing a different model and outfit composition with garment chips.

**By the numbers.** Four key metrics: minus 38% returns, plus 24% add-to-cart, 1.4-second render, 97% match accuracy. (These are placeholder figures to be confirmed.)

**For brands.** An editorial section with a fabric image and six feature highlights: cloth-aware rendering, full outfit composition, brand-faithful colour, PDP embedding, privacy by design, and a merchandiser console.

**Integrations.** Four platform cards: Shopify (theme app embed), Salesforce Commerce Cloud (cartridge), Magento (extension), and custom storefronts (script tag).

**Pricing.** Three tiers — Atelier (from GBP 490/mo, up to 200 SKUs), Maison (from GBP 1,490/mo, up to 2,000 SKUs, couture-grade rendering), and Haute (custom, unlimited SKUs, dedicated account team). Pricing is placeholder.

**FAQ.** Seven questions brands commonly ask, from rendering accuracy to photograph privacy to integration timelines.

**Final CTA.** "See the look. Then decide." with buttons to open the dressing room or speak to the team, plus a newsletter email capture.

**Footer.** The Vestra manifesto, navigation links, contact details, and links to Privacy and Terms pages.

## The virtual fitting room (try-on page)

This is the core product demo. The page opens with a heading: "Compose the look. See it on you."

**Step 1 — Choose a shopper.** The visitor picks from three preset shoppers (sample images) or uploads their own photograph.

**Step 2 — Build an outfit.** Garments are shown in a grid, organised by category (outerwear, tops, bottoms, shoes, accessories). The visitor selects pieces to build a complete look. Three one-click presets are available: Casual, Professional, and Night Out.

**Step 3 — Generate.** When the visitor clicks "Generate look," an iridescent shimmer fills the canvas while the AI renders the outfit. A progress indicator shows the rendering stage.

**The result.** A result card appears with the rendered image, a confidence score, and three action buttons: Save (downloads the image), Share (copies a link), and Shop the Look (scrolls to the garment list). A small "Preview" badge indicates this is a demo rendering.

**Outfit variations.** Below the result, alternative garment suggestions are offered so the visitor can swap pieces and re-render.

A photo retention notice is displayed at the point where shoppers upload, explaining that photographs are processed in-session only and never stored for training.

## Other pages

**Contact.** A form with fields for name, house/brand, email, role, catalogue size, and message. Submitting shows a confirmation.

**Privacy.** A full privacy policy covering data collection, photograph processing, GDPR rights, India DPDP compliance, cookies, and data retention.

**Terms.** Terms of service covering service description, user responsibilities, intellectual property, limitation of liability, and governing law.

---

## What is a demo vs. what is real

The fitting room on this site uses sample images and a simulated rendering process. The "Preview" badge makes this clear. For a live pilot with your own catalogue and real-time AI rendering, the Vestra team will set up a private atelier for your brand.
